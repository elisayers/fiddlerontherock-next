Questions

Everything you need
to know.

Is this really private — just the two of us?
Yes. Completely. No other guests, no other groups, no strangers. The canyon location is selected for you. Tyler performs exclusively for you for the full serenade. The photographer is there only for you. This is not a shared experience.
Are children allowed?
Sedona Serenades is designed exclusively for adult couples. For families or groups with children, Tyler's Red Rock Nature Concert is a wonderful option.
How far in advance should we book?
A minimum of 5 days is required. Fall season books 4–6 weeks out. Valentine's Day and New Year's Eve are exclusive one-couple-per-year dates at a separate price point — those require direct inquiry well in advance. If you have a specific date in mind, reach out as soon as possible.
What if it rains?
Sedona averages over 300 sunny days per year. In the rare event of weather, Tyler will work with you to reschedule at no additional cost.
How far do we walk?
The primary Sedona Serenades location is a short, easy walk from parking — approximately 5 minutes on a gentle path. If mobility is a consideration, let us know when you inquire and Tyler will select the accessible Cultural Park location.
What should we wear?
Comfortable layers — Sedona evenings shift quickly. Closed-toe shoes for the short walk. A wide-brimmed hat for daytime sessions. You'll look stunning in the photographs regardless.
Can Tyler learn our song?
Yes — Tyler offers a "Your Song on Violin" add-on for $250 per song. He'll learn and perform your chosen melody live during the serenade. Select this option when you inquire and he'll be in touch to confirm the arrangement.
